Getting Started

Download My Six Degrees and register with your name, occupation, and postal code. You'll appear on the global network map instantly.

Share your personal invite link to connect with friends and watch your network spread across the globe.

Restore Your Account

If you reinstall the app, use the "Already have an account?" option on the welcome screen and enter your User ID to restore your network.

Your User ID was shown after you first registered. If you've lost it, contact us and we'll help you recover it.

Your Network Map

Your map shows up to six degrees of connections color-coded by distance — green for close, white for far. Tap any pin to see the exact path connecting you.

Hit Refresh Map to load the latest connections. The map updates automatically as new people join your network.
